The music in this app is brought to you by Radio Hyrule. It is a fan project owned and operated by members of the Zelda Reorchestrated Team. Our mission is to provide rising musicians an outlet to display their creative musical works while providing Zelda fans with an entertaining online radio station.
Learn more

By purchasing this app you helped to cover the costs of hosting and maintaining this service. If you want to help even more, you can donate on our website.
